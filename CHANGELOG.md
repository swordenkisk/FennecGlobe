## [4.2.0] - 2026-03-02

- Full satellite orbit visualization with 3 animated orbital rings
- FennecAI assistant with context-aware geopolitical intelligence
- 4 complete themes: Algerian Green, Dark Blue, Light, Cyber Purple
- Social intelligence feed panel
- Active alerts panel with severity classification
- Live animated ticker bar with 12 market instruments
- Bookmark sidebar with categorized feeds
- Full settings panel (Display, Notifications, Data, Privacy)
- Google + Apple + Email authentication flow
- Live counter animations on hero stats
- Load more news functionality
- Breaking news rotating bar
- Keyboard shortcuts (/, Ctrl+K, Esc)
- Mobile responsive layout
- Scroll-to-top button
- Contact floating buttons
- Map layer toggles (Conflicts, Weather, Shipping, Flights, Military, Economics, Disease)
- Weather °C/°F toggle
- Markets cards/table view toggle
- Toast notification system
- Launch animation with particle effects and status cycling
